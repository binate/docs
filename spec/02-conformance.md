## Scope notes

- Compiler and interpreter are co-equal implementations. Cross-mode agreement requirement: where both modes coexist they must agree on observable layout/behavior; impl-defined choices (e.g. word size) must agree.
- Hosted vs freestanding conformance (bare-metal stays conformant).
- Full in-process embedding is a GOAL, not asserted realized (see Ch.19).
